Building Scenarios: The 3R Principle

Note

Effective scenarios are not point forecasts‚Äîthey are structured narratives that help the Board reason about uncertainty and policy trade-offs. The 3R Principle guides this process: Realistic narratives grounded in data and institutions, Ranges (not single numbers) for key variables, and attention to potential Regime shifts that can change how the economy behaves.

Using 3R scenarios ensures policy is robust to alternative paths for inflation, output, and financial conditions. This approach supports FPAS Mark II‚Äôs least-regrets mindset by linking policy choices to transparent assumptions and measurable risks‚Äîso decisions can adapt as new information arrives.
