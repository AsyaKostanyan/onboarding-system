Principles 1 & 2:
A Clear Nominal Anchor & Consistency Between Objectives

Note

Principles 1 and 2 form the foundation of modern inflation-targeting frameworks: a clear nominal anchor and strong internal consistency across all policy objectives. Together, they ensure that policy actions, communication, and institutional design all reinforce price stability. These principles became essential after the failures of monetary targeting and the lessons learned from the Great Inflation of the 1970s.

A credible nominal anchor not only stabilizes expectations ‚Äî it also aligns every component of the policy framework. Armenia‚Äôs modern approach under FPAS Mark II places these two principles at the core of its decision-making process.
