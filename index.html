Monetary Policy and the Evolution of Modern Central Banking

Welcome to the Central Bank of Armenia’s Onboarding Platform for new Board Members. This platform introduces you to the evolution of modern monetary policy — from its global intellectual roots to Armenia’s current framework, known as the Prudent Risk Management Approach to Price Stability (PRMAPS), also referred to as FPAS Mark II.

Designed for newly appointed members of the CBA Board, the platform provides a structured and intellectually engaging journey through the principles and practice of central banking. It explains how economic thinking evolved through decades of global experience, culminating in the CBA’s modern approach to policy under uncertainty.

Program Format

The onboarding consists of 21 concise videos (each 5–10 minutes long), each accompanied by a short explanatory note, background readings, and Armenia-specific insights. The tone is conceptual and intuitive — focused on building shared understanding and policy intuition rather than technical mastery.

Learning Journey

  • From the Great Inflation of the 1970s to the birth of inflation targeting.
  • Principles of effective monetary frameworks — anchors, credibility, and consistency.
  • The CBA’s transition to FPAS Mark II and the Least-Regrets mindset.
  • Scenario-based policymaking, communication, and accountability.

Each section connects global lessons with Armenia’s experience, demonstrating how theory meets practice inside the CBA.

Purpose

The goal of this platform is to build a shared mindset for decision-making under uncertainty. Understanding monetary policy at the CBA is not just about economics — it is about strengthening collective judgment, transparency, and credibility in pursuit of price stability.
